Ontome Profiles

OntoMe Profiles are collections of classes and properties related to a specific topic.

Example:

  • Bibliographic profile
    The bibliographic profile bundles classes and properties perfect to describe the life of persons.

OntoMe itself is an open platform for the development, management and sharing of ontologies.
